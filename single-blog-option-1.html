Hotel Not Hotel : Unique Stay in Amsterdam

Hotel Not Hotel is located in Amsterdam West, more specificly in the upcoming neighbourhood De Baarsjes. Maybe it's not the first location you would think of when looking for a hotel in Amsterdam. However, staying at Hotel Not Hotel in Amsterdam will assure that you have a true local experience. You'll find Hotel Not Hotel in a residential neighborhood, and it's the only hotel in that area.

Is Hotel Not Hotel a hotel? Or is it one of Amsterdam’s newest restaurant and bar hot spots? You decide!

This new and trendy hotel is located in a beautiful building with classical arches and an industrial finish. Very special about the hotel are the rooms! All the rooms are individually created by Collaboration-O, a group of designers from the famous Eindhoven Design Academy.

Have you ever heard of a place where you can spend the night in an antique Amsterdam tram cart? Or in a classic Volkswagen minivan, Tintin’s red and white rocket or one of the other luxury cottages. That sounds really special, doesn’t it? The rooms are spectacular, but since they're fully aimed at a theme, the bathroom is shared.

At Hotel Not Hotel in Amsterdam West, the staff aims to offer you a glimpse at the real Amsterdam with insider tips about nightlife, food or cultural happenings. The hotel also has a very popular restaurant and bar. At the Hotel Not Hotel's bar, you can wind down after exploring the city and meet locals while you enjoy one of the fine cheeses and a matching beer, wine or maybe even a whiskey.

If you're looking for a special place to host a party, Hotel Not Hotel is a great spot. You can rent the whole hotel for the night and stay there with your friends. Imagine dancing the night away and then rolling into bed only to enjoy breakfast with your friends the next day.

The bar and restaurant at Hotel Not Hotel is super trendy and very popular with locals. They use it as their favorite new lunch spot and place to meet with friends for drinks in the afternoon and evening.

Another great thing about Hotel Not Hotel is the fact that they offer bikes for rent. Amsterdam is a perfect city to discover by bike since there are bike lanes everywhere. Or order the hotel boat to make a trip on the canals during the warm weather months.

Yes, Hotel Not Hotel offers it all!
